Synchronisation

You can backup the credentials from your device to a file or cloud storage, and restore them later in case your Mooltipass device gets lost or broken. Your credentials will always been encrypted with your personal security key, which is only stored at your key cards. You are secure, even if someone gets access to your backup files.

File Storage

Store your credentials in a file on your local computer.

Cloud Storage

Store your credentials in your Google account. Your backup can be accessed from all Chrome browsers where you are logged in.
